Traditional Menemen Recipe

A classic Turkish breakfast made with eggs, tomatoes, peppers, and olive oil ‚Äî simple, warm, and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 green peppers, finely chopped
  • 3 ripe tomatoes, grated or chopped
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional spices (red pepper flakes, black pepper)

Cooking Guide

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© green peppers until softened.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until thickened.
  4. Season with salt and spices.
  5. Crack eggs into the pan.
  6. Cook gently until eggs are just set.
  7. Serve hot with fresh bread.
